Data Deletion Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026

This page explains VidyaDham Mandir data deletion policy for users of our mobile app and related services.

1. Student Data Deletion Requests

Student-initiated data deletion requests are not allowed. Student data is maintained as an institutional digital reference for managing attendance, fees, timetables, marks, and academic history.

2. How Deletion Is Controlled

  • Deletion is used only when records are no longer required by institute policy.

3. Data Retention Reason

Core records are retained because they function as the official digital reference for student management and compliance with institute operations.
