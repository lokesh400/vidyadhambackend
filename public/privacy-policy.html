Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VidyaDham Mandir ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Depending on your role (student, teacher, admin, receptionist, staff), we may collect:

  • Account information: name, username, email address, role, account status.
  • Student profile details: roll number, mobile number, father name, mother name, address, profile photo, batch.
  • Academic and attendance data: timetables, attendance records, test marks/results.
  • Fee and payment records: fee components, payment amount, payment mode, receipt details, payment date.
  • Enquiry and follow-up data: student name, mobile number, query description, remarks, follow-up notes.
  • Form submission data: form responses, name, email, mobile number, submission timestamps.
  • Staff and recruitment data (admin-side): employee details and uploaded documents (for example Aadhaar, PAN, resume, offer/experience letters, photos, job descriptions).
  • Authentication data: encrypted password credentials, session information, OTP records for password reset.

2. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage user accounts and access control.
  • Provide academic administration features (attendance, marks, timetables, fees, forms, admit cards).
  • Send service communications such as OTP, account credentials, timetable updates, form confirmations, and admit card notifications.
  • Maintain records for institute operations and support.
  • Improve platform reliability, debugging, and security.

3. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone.

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies, Sessions, and Login

The App uses authenticated sessions and cookies to keep users logged in securely. Session data may be stored in our database for up to 7 days.

5. Data Retention

  • Account and educational records are kept while your account is active or as required by institute operations.
  • We retain core student data as a digital reference for managing students, attendance, fees, timetables, and academic history.
  • Password reset OTP records automatically expire (about 5 minutes).
  • Session records are retained for a limited period (about 7 days).
  • Uploaded files/documents are retained until deleted by administrators or no longer required.

5A. Data Deletion Requests

Data can be deleted by authorized administrators using our deletion endpoint /data/delete. This is used when records are no longer required, while balancing the need to keep institutional data as a digital reference for student management. Students cannot request deletion of records directly.

6. Data Security

We use role-based access controls, authenticated sessions, and standard security practices to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children and Student Data

This App is intended for educational institute operations and may process student data, including minors, under school/institute authorization and applicable law.

8. Your Rights

You can request access and correction of your data by contacting us using the details below. Student-initiated data deletion requests are not allowed.
